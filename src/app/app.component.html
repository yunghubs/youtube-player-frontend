Welcome to the ReliefApps YouTube player.

Take the URL of your favorite YouTube video and paste it into the search bar. Please make sure that your URL ends with the specific YouTube ID (eleven characters after "v=" in the URL) for every video. If you click the „Show Video“ button, the video will open and you can watch it. The link of the video will be added automatically into your history. From the history you can also watch all your videos again if you click on the URL. By clicking the „Add to my Bookmarks“ button you can add the currently loaded video to your personal bookmarks. You can also display your bookmarks by clicking „Show my Bookmarks“. By clicking again your list of bookmarks disappears again. A counter shows how many videos you already have added to your bookmarks.
