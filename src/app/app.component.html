Welcome to the ReliefApps YouTube player.

Take the the URL of your favorite YouTube video and paste it into the search bar of this page. Please make sure your URL ends with the specific YouTube ID for every video. If you click the „Show Video“ Button, the video opens and you can watch it. The link of the Video will be added into your History list automatically. From there you can also rewatch the Video if you click on one of the items in our History list. By Clicking the „Add to my Bookmarks“ button you can ad the current Video into a List of your personal Bookmarks and can open the bookmarks list by clicking „Show my Bookmarks“.
